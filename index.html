UWE Coding Club 2016-2017

Join The Society by becoming a member here

The UWE Coding Club is a chance for programmers, new and old, to get together in an informal setting to learn, work on ###projects and share/collaborate with other members.

Doesn’t matter if you’re a programming expert or have no knowledge at all, come along, get involved with a project or ###just have a chat!

There will also be small tutorials each week for beginners, or members, wishing to learn a new language.
